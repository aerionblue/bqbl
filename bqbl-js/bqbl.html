Disclaimers

Our scraper miscounts certain things that earn points in BQBL. All of these will be fixed manually in the tracking spreadsheet later:

  • Fumbles returned for TD (+10 points) — these are counted in 'fumbles lost', so the total score will be off by 5
  • QB sacked for a safety (+20 points)
  • Game-winning drive (−12 points)
  • Game-losing INT-TD in OT (+50 points)
  • Non-injury mid-game benching (+35 points) — this is a judgment call that will be added later
  • All 24/7 points — we can only automatically determine on-the-field points
  • Passes thrown by non-QBs (e.g., punters in a fake FG) are counted, but they shouldn't be

We also differ from Grantland (and the NFL box score) in that we deduct sack yardage from the QB passing yard total.
